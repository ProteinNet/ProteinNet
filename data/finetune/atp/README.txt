In this folder, you can find two txt format files, which contain the protein-ATP binding samples of PATP-388 and PATP-TEST datasets, respecitively.

Taking PATP-TEST as an example, its ATP-binding site information can be found in the "PATP-TEST_list.txt". In this file, each line represents one protein information as the following format.

Protein name : Number of ATP-binding sites : ATP-binding sites : Protein sequence

e.g.,
5A98A : 7 : E10 Y14 K20 R21 F23 E152 D153 : MEDYTLREREQEIYNRQVIKRPFDPDSYLTADLHLYLKNGKTLTIHIERNLFFSHEFTWEEICRGKCDTQEYIDGLVADNGGRSTHNSSYLEPVAFQLTLLGNFDLGSIHLRIGDYLGFRDGQRFPCKETIHGRRDTIGPFMQGMSGKWAKEDYIHTYSGRFDCKTSRHPSIFLAFMRANQDGHSSFAPENMRNALLYSGDKSPRYILMDNEHTLINNFIIPRCLPYRDQYGKDY


Reference: Jun Hu, Yang Li, Dongjun Yu, and Yang Zhang, ATPbind: accurate protein-ATP binding site prediction by combining sequence-profiling and structure-based comparison. XXXXX, 2017 (Submitted).
